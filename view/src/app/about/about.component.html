HOW

Our fine yet humble folk sit at recycled timber desks, work on laptops powered by rechargeable batteries and never misuse electricity in and around studio. They breathe fresh air created by trees around/plants grown in-house and let the windows open for sunlight; allowing AC/Coolers to RIP. We avoid glass cabins at our best. These ‘Digitoons’ hold meetings within a cave of recycled paper laced walls; reusing paper to its last shred (we still don’t know what a paper shredder is! ). Though white board scribbles are more appreciated these days. We said no to plastic in pantry and on desk to maximum most. When it’s time to leave, the team grab their bags, switch upcycled electricity extension boards off and they flick the switch to shut the LED’s powered panels. Back to home; they walk, ride and catch public transport. That’s the commitment!
